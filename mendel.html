About

Mendelbalm is a middle-class makeup brand crafted to give everyday beauty lovers a touch of effortless upper-class elegance. Its clean, refined packaging and rich, silky formulas create a luxurious experience without the luxury price tag. Mendelbalm lets anyone feel polished, poised, and quietly high-end.

The Mendelbalm brand was built using primarily photoshop and after effects.

Website Objective

Mendelbalm’s website aims to present accessible luxury by showcasing refined, high-quality makeup that helps everyday users feel effortlessly elegant.
